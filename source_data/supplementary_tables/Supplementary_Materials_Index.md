## Supplementary Figure 1

**Supplementary Figure 1. Detailed cellular localization of locked immune-reactive component scores in GSE266919.** Standardized HLA-II, CXCL9/FOLR2 macrophage, and dendritic-cell/HLA module scores are shown across selected marker-coherent immune subclusters. Rows are grouped by broad cellular compartment. Color denotes the standardized module score; blank cells indicate unavailable or non-scoreable combinations. These analyses describe cell-source localization and were not used as patient-level response validation.
